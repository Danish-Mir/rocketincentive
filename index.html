Humans of Nova Scotia is a podcast that uncovers the unique stories and experiences of people across the province.

  • Narrative:  Through diverse narratives—from healthcare workers to artists and everyday heroes—the podcast fosters community connection by celebrating resilience, innovation, and kindness.
  • Growth Plan:  As it grows, Humans of Nova Scotia will evolve into a fundraising platform, using storytelling to raise awareness and support for local charities and social initiatives.
  • Podcast Goal:   Each episode becomes a vehicle for positive change, combining personal stories with a greater purpose of giving back to the community and making a lasting impact.

Our Goal and Motivation

Our goal is to share Nova Scotia's unique stories, fostering connection and compassion across the community. Motivated by a commitment to positive change, we aim to inspire support and celebrate resilience through every voice we amplify.

  • Stories that Inspire:  To bring hidden stories to light, highlighting the resilience and kindness within our communities.
  • A Platform for Change:  To create a platform that not only tells stories but also drives meaningful support for local causes.
  • Everyday Heroes:  To empower everyday heroes by sharing their journeys, inspiring others to connect and contribute.

Our Commitment to the Community

At Humans of Nova Scotia, our commitment goes beyond storytelling. We strive to create a space where individuals feel seen, heard, and empowered to inspire change in their communities.

  • Building Connections:  We aim to bring people together by fostering meaningful conversations and shared experiences.
  • Raising Awareness:  We shine a light on untold stories, tackling important issues and celebrating resilience.
  • Empowering Voices:  We provide a platform for individuals from diverse backgrounds to share their journeys and inspire action.
